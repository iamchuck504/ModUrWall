Questions

What does "on-device generation" mean? +
Your device runs the AI model locally — no internet required, no data leaves your phone. It uses a lightweight Stable Diffusion 1.5 model optimized for mobile. Results are good, but server-side generation (Pro/Studio) produces significantly better quality and resolution.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel through your account settings or the app store. Your Pro/Studio features remain active until the end of your billing period. No penalties, no questions asked.
How does Lempyrλ authentication work? +
Paid tiers authenticate through Lempyrλ's ClearingHouse OAuth 2.0 system. Your credentials are secured with quantum-generated keys and JWT tokens. ModUrWall never stores your password — all auth is delegated to Lempyrλ's infrastructure.
What happens to my wallpapers if I downgrade? +
Wallpapers saved to your device stay yours forever. Cloud gallery items remain accessible in read-only mode for 30 days after downgrade, giving you time to download anything you need.
Is there an App Store in-app purchase option? +
Yes. On iOS and Android, subscriptions are handled through the respective app stores' in-app purchase systems. Web subscriptions go directly through our payment portal with Stripe, which means better pricing (no 30% store cut).
